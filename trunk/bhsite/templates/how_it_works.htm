How Insurance from Bitcoin Volatility Works:


  1. Specify insurance parameters
    (expiry, size, and whether to protect from increase or decrease in bitcoin rate)

  2. Provide your bitcoin address. Your insurance payout will be sent there upon expiry.

  3. Press "Submit Order" button.
    Our bitcoin address will be displayed as a result.

  4. Make your fee payment to our address within 5 minutes
    (otherwise, your order will be cancelled).
    If you pay a differen amount than the quote, your order will still be opened, but the size will be adjusted proportionally.
    For example, if the fee quoted to you for insuring 1 bitcoin was 0.02, but you have paid only 0.01,
    then a contract to ensure only 0.5 bitcoins will be opened.

  5. After your contract expires
    If a payment is due to you, it will be sent to your bitcoin address within a minute.

Use Cases

  • Bitcoin holders protect their savings.
  • Bitcoin debtors protect against increase in bitcoin price to repay debt.
  • Speculators try to buy insurance cheaply and receive a large payout.
