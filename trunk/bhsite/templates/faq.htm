Frequently Asked Questions

Who uses your products?

Businesses and individuals can take advantage of our risk management products, e.g.:
  • Bitcoin holders protect their savings and investments.
  • Bitcoin debtors protect themselves against increase in their debt levels due to adverse exchange rate movements.
  • Speculators attempt to profit by buying our products cheaply and receiving large payouts.

What new features are upcoming to the web site?

Login accounts will be introduced in the near future.
They will allow users to access all their order information and history on a single page.
This will also allow users to deposit funds in order to use marginable products.

What are Marginable Products?

Our contracts, in which upon expiry the user may end up owing us money are called marginable products.
The user is required to post margin funds prior to entering into such contracts.
We plan to introduce such contracts in 2014Q4.

What new products do you plan to introduce?

Currently, we offer two products:
     1. Protection against a DECREASE in BTC/USD exchange rate (useful for bitcoin holders).
     2. Protection against an INCREASE in BTC/USD exchange rate (useful for bitcoin borrowers).

We will be gradually introducing more products, including:
  • More currency pairs, not only BTC/USD.
  • Strike price ladder, which will allow users to specify the desired opening exchange rate for contracts.
  • Futures contracts
  • Long and short positions, allowing the users to both buy or sell the contracts.

How do I know that you will be able to pay me after my order expires?

We plan to make our reserve levels verifiable and fully transparent to the users.

How do you manage your own risk?

We will use various tools and techniques available for managing our business risks, including:
  • Derivatives markets instruments to neutralize our own exposure to the markets.
  • Real-time discount offers on those types of orders, which offset our current risk exposures.
  • Re-insuring the risks of expecially large orders, which are not prudent to bear ourselves.
Currently, you see tight limits on the allowed order sizes.
As our risk management framework completes rigorous testing, these order size limits will be greatly relaxed or lifted completely.
