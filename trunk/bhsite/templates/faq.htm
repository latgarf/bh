Frequently Asked Questions

Who uses your products?

Several categories of individual and business users take advantage of our risk management products, including:
  • Bitcoin holders like to protect their savings and investments.
  • Bitcoin debtors like to protect against increase in their debt levels due to adverse exchange rate movements.
  • Speculators like to profit by buying our products cheaply and receiving large payouts.

What new features are upcoming to the web site?

Signup/login accounts will be introduced in the near future.
They will allow the users to access all their order information and history on a single page.
This will also allow the users to deposit funds in order to use marginable products.

What are Marginable Products?

The contracts, where upon expiry the user may owe us funds are called marginable products.
They require the user to post margin funds prior to entering into such contracts.
We plan to introduce such contracts in 2014Q4.

What new products do you plan to introduce?

Currently, we offer two products:
  1. Protection against a DECREASE in BTC/USD exchange rate (useful for bitcoin holders).
  2. Protection against an INCREASE in BTC/USD exchange rate (useful for bitcoin borrowers).
We will gradually introduce more products, including:
  • More currency pairs, not only BTC/USD.
  • Strike price ladder, which will allow users to specify the desired opening exchange rate for contracts.
  • Futures contracts
  • Long and short positions, allowing the users to both buy or sell the contracts.

How do I know that you will be able to pay me after my order expires?

We plan to make our reserve levels verifiable and fully transparent.

How do you manage your own risk?

We use various tools and techniques available for managing our business risks, including:
  • Available market instruments to neutralize our own exposure to the markets.
  • Offering real-time discounts on those types of orders, which offset our current risk exposures.
  • Re-insure large order risks, which are not prudent to bear ourselves.
Currently, you see very tight limits on the size of the orders that can be placed.
These order size limits will be greatly relaxed or totally lifted in the future as our risk management framework completes rigorous testing.
